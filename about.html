Established in 1994
Fabricated Furniture and Design was established in 1994 as a family owned wholesale furniture business. Since then it has changed ownership and now specialises in sustainable CNC produced furniture and 3D printing. The vision of Fabricated Furnitue and Design is to to reduce environmental impacts of traditional methods of carpentry and 3D printing - we hope to be the premier furniture & design business in Australia specialising in reclaimed wood products and environmentally friendly plastics.
